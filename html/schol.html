Top Schemes

Pradhan Mantri KISAN Samman Nidhi(PM-KISAN)

The PM AASHA (Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay SanraksHan Abhiyan) scheme is a government initiative aimed at ensuring farmers receive fair prices for their produce. It includes mechanisms like price support, price deficiency payments, and procurement to safeguard farmers' incomes.Launched to ensure fair prices for farmers, the PM AASHA scheme includes price support, deficiency payments.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a crop insurance scheme launched in 2016 to protect farmers from financial losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases. It covers crop losses and shares the premium cost between the government and farmers, reducing their vulnerability and stabilizing the agricultural sector.

Explore
Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY) is a centrally sponsored scheme launched in 2015 to support farmers in installing micro-irrigation systems. It provides financial assistance through interest-free loans for the first two years, benefiting farmers with less than 2 hectares of land. The scheme improves water use efficiency, boosts crop production, and enhances farmers' income, promoting sustainable agriculture.

The Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) is a specialized agency of the United Nations that leads international efforts to defeat hunger and improve nutrition. Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan

Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan (KKA): Launched in 2018, KKA is a centrally sponsored scheme targeting aspirational districts to enhance farmers' income and livelihoods through activities like soil health card distribution, livestock vaccination, and organic farming promotion. The scheme has trained over 11 lakh farmers and improved soil health on 15 lakh hectares of land.

Green Revolution – Krishonnati Yojana

The Green Revolution – Krishonnati Yojana is an umbrella program launched by the Indian government to enhance agricultural productivity and ensure food security. It includes various sub-schemes focused on modernizing agriculture through technological advancements, improved irrigation, and better crop management practices. The program aims to support farmers by increasing crop yields and income, contributing to the overall growth of the agricultural sector.

EParamparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana

The Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) is an initiative by the Indian government aimed at promoting organic farming across the country. Launched in 2015 under the National Mission for Agriculture, it encourages farmers to adopt traditional organic practices and helps them market their organic produce.

National Mission on Natural Farming

The National Mission on Natural Farming (NMNF) is a government initiative aimed at promoting natural farming practices across India. Launched to reduce dependency on chemical inputs, the mission focuses on enhancing soil fertility, conserving resources, and ensuring sustainable agriculture It encourages farmers use traditional, eco-friendly farming methods .

Government Schemes

Pradhan Mantri KISAN Samman Nidhi(PM-KISAN)

The PM AASHA (Pradhan Mantri Annadata Aay SanraksHan Abhiyan) scheme is a government initiative aimed at ensuring farmers receive fair prices for their produce. It includes mechanisms like price support, price deficiency payments, and procurement to safeguard farmers' incomes.Launched to ensure fair prices for farmers, this scheme supports procurement and price support mechanisms.

Soil Health Management (SHM) Scheme

The Soil Health Management (SHM) Scheme focuses on improving soil health through various measures such as soil testing, organic farming practices, and the promotion of integrated nutrient management. It aims to enhance soil fertility and agricultural productivity.

National Agriculture Market (eNAM)

The eNAM (National Agriculture Market) is an online trading platform that aims to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities. It facilitates transparent price discovery, efficient trading, and better market access for farmersIt facilitates transparent price discovery, efficient trading, and better market access for farmers.

Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS)

The Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) Scheme provides a range of services aimed at improving the health and nutrition of children and pregnant women. It includes supplementary nutrition, health check-ups, and early childhood education programs.

Explore
Rural Skill Development Schemes

The National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (NREGA) provides job security and income to rural households through guaranteed employment on public works projects. It promotes sustainable livelihoods, community asset creation, and enhances rural infrastructure, benefiting marginalized and disadvantaged groups.

Explore
Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY) aims to provide financial inclusion to all households by offering accessible banking services. It includes features such as zero-balance accounts, overdraft facilities, and insurance coverage, improving financial access for the economically disadvantaged.
